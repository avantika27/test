B.VOC WEB DESIGNING

Scope of the Subject


The programme aims to build individual capacities and train persons with adequate employability skills. The programme structure attempts to blend appropriate technical knowledge and skills, personal and professional skills and substantive ‘hands-on’ and field / site experience required in the trade. Keeping in view the demands of the market and to provide flexible options for students the programme is designed in modular manner and allows entry and exit options at various levels. The learners will have flexibility to develop themselves according to their strengths and career interests. The student will get job in the market according to the NAQF level. In case of B.Voc (Web Designing), student will get job as a Web Developer after certification of NSQF Level -5 (Job Role – Web Developer) and award of Diploma in Web Designing. B.Vov (Web Designing) students will get Web Designer job after NSQF Level – 5, Senior Web Designer after NSQF Level -6 and Software Developer or Data Associate after NSQF Level – 7. The students of B.Vov (Web Designing) are eligible to join Post Graduate Courses as well as join any of the government job like IAS, SSC, State Public Service Commission, Banks, Railways and so forth. The students of B.Vov may start their own business in their respective expertise/field.
